About Kim

Kim is a man whose mere existence can be termed as a "blessing to the whole of humanity.". this was not the case always. Kim hails from a humble background and has risen to become one of the world's greatest innovators.

Kim's achievements

Kim is a great person and innovator. He has created some of the applications that even our descendants will use since they are priceless and timeless. From the above reasons we can actually justify that it was only right that Kim deserved to earn himself Nobel Peace Prize in the year 2016 in the field of innovation. To everyones suprise Kim found himself again bagging a Nobel Peace Prize the next year because of his humanity. This put him in the history books as the first person to ever win two Nobel Peace Prizes and further more, he did so in two consecutive years.
